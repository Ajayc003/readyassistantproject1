Bounce-Infinity

The Bounce Infinity E1 electric scooter has been launched in India. It is the Bengaluru-based ride-sharing startup’s first electric offering in the country, priced at Rs 68,999 (for the variant with the battery pack) and Rs 45,099 (for the one without). Both prices, ex-showroom Delhi. It is also the first e-scooter in India to be sold with and without the battery pack. The latter comes with a paid subscription scheme, wherein you’ll have to pay to swap the battery from one the company’s upcoming swapping network. More details in our launch report.
